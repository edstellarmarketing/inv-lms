12.2
Two independent gates

Pass Foundation → unlock Practitioner gate

Foundation gate (composite ≥ 70) unlocks first. He'd book Foundation, pass, then Practitioner becomes the next focus (composite ≥ 75, higher bar). Each leg has its own validity window: F expires earlier, P later to give him runway.

Money-back across the combo: If he passes F but fails P, he can claim the P portion of the fee back (gate-honoured). The guarantee applies per attempt, not per combo.
